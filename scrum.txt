1. DEFINITION OF SCRUM
	Scrum is a process framework in which people can address complex adaptive problems. 
	Scrum is 
	a). lightweight, b). simply to understand, c). difficult to master.
It is a process in which one can employ various processes and techniques.
It helps in continuously improving the product, the team, and the working environment.
Scrum framework consists of scrum teams and their associated roles, events, artifacts and rules.
